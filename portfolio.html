Animousity

Animousity is a project to recommend anime movies and TV shows to anyone who is interested in anime, but does not know where to start. Do you like movies? That's all we need! Animousity takes a user-suggested movie, and will return anime suggestions based on criteria like the year, and genre of your movie. Happy anime-hunting
